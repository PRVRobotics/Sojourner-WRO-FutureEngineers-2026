#### Hardware Transition
- Switched from EV3 motors to LEGO Spike Prime system
- Reasons for transition:
  - Improved motor efficiency and precision
  - Better size optimization (more compact hub)
  - Higher control accuracy for autonomous driving
- Wheel configuration updated:
  - Front: LEGO Spike wheels (better maneuverability)
  - Back: Wide EV3 wheels (improved traction)
